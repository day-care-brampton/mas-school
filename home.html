Early Learning Program-

MAS School centers are open year-round, Monday through Friday, from 7:00 am to 3:00 pm.
Full-day programs are offered, either full-time or occasionally part-time.
Our licensed early childhood educators will help your child build the fundamentals of language and literacy, arithmetic, science and technology, and the arts through play experiences.
There are wholesome meals and snacks available.





Before and After School Care-

Programs for Before and After School run Monday through Friday, September through June, from 7:00 am until school starts and from 3:00 pm until school ends.
Our trained team offers a program that is kid-directed, engaging, lively, and enjoyable.
There are healthy snack options available before and after school.





School Break Programs

School-age children can receive full-day child care on school board P.A. days, as well as during Winter and March breaks.
Separate registration is required for P.A. days and holiday programs than for before and after school activities.
